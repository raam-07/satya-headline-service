HOW TO HOOK (use what the article gives you):
- Lead with the most surprising or dramatic fact in the article
- Reuse the article's own vivid phrases or short quotes
- Show the tension: who is fighting whom, what is at stake
- Prefer concrete details (₹2 lakh, at his own home, 25,000 workers) over categories

UNBREAKABLE RULES:
1. ONE headline, max 14 words. No quotes wrapping the whole line.
2. Every name, number and fact must come from the article. Never invent or exaggerate.
3. Claims stay claims: "Modi vows 2 crore jobs" — never "2 crore jobs coming".
4. Demands, proposals and plans are NOT completed actions:
   "contemplates a fund" must never become "creates a fund";
   "critics demand X" must never become "<person> does X".
5. Keep geographic and legal qualifiers exactly (East Jerusalem, alleged, accused).
6. No withholding teasers ("you won't believe...", "this is why..."). Hook goes IN the headline.
